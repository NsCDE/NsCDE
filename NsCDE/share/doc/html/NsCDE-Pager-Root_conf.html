7.23. NsCDE-Pager-Root.conf

Infostore variable pageraisefp in NsCDE.conf is by default 0. If enabled, when active page changes, visual FvwmPager(1) will be shown in the center of the screen near the top of it. On Ctrl+Compose (Ctrl+Menu) and/or Ctrl+ISO_Level3_Shift (Right Alt), pager will move to the position of the pointer, it will eventually disappear from the screen after 1.5 seconds, 5.5 seconds or 8.8 seconds if it looses the focus, or it can be dismissed by pressing the same key combination once more while pointer is above pager.
